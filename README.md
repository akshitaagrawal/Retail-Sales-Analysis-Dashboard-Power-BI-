Category Performance Power BI Dashboard

Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes product category, subcategory, and regional sales data to deliver actionable business insights for management. Users can interactively filter results by category and region, and dynamically track performance metrics such as Revenue, Profit, and Profit Margin over time. The solution demonstrates advanced data visualization and dynamic DAX measures to support both strategic and operational decision-making.

Features

- Category & Region Selection: Allows granular analysis using slicers to explore specific product categories and geographical regions.
- Revenue Contribution by Subcategory: Horizontal bar charts visualize which subcategories drive the most revenue within a chosen category and region.
- Monthly Revenue and Profit Trends: Area charts track both revenue and profit across months, helping managers identify trends and seasonality.
- Subcategory Performance Table: Provides a breakdown of each subcategoryâ€™s revenue, profit, and profit percentage, with totals and averages for quick benchmarking.
- Supplier Analysis: Lists suppliers for the chosen category/region, revealing contributor performance and profit margins to enable procurement decisions.
- Management Scorecard: Quarterly performance cards show metrics for Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin, and Units Sold in a concise, easy-to-present format.
- Product & Regional Performance: Trend lines help compare revenue growth for products and regions, using visually distinct color schemes.

Analysis Summary

- Cameras and Video Equipment consistently contribute the highest percentage of revenue within the Electronics category in the South region.
- Profit margins are stable across subcategories, with Computers showing slightly lower profitability due to higher costs.
- Monthly trend analysis reveals an uptick in revenue and profit in Q4, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- Supplier analysis highlights ACS Innovations and Entertaintron Inc. as top partners for both revenue and margin.
- Quarterly scorecards show upward trends, underscoring business growth and opportunities for market expansion.

Product Performance Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of product trends and sales performance over time. Users can switch between monthly and quarterly analysis at the category and product level. Slicers for country, category, and metric empower users to drill down and compare product outcomes, informing strategic sales and marketing decisions.

Key Components:

- Revenue Performance: Line charts visualize revenue per product across time, showing seasonality and growth patterns. Users can toggle between monthly and quarterly views for granular or summary insights.
- Product Revenue Distribution: Filterable bar/line visualizations spotlight top-performing products, enabling focused performance reviews and identifying market leaders.
- Regional Revenue Performance: Multi-line charts allow comparison of multiple regions, revealing geographic trends and sales hotspots. This supports targeted strategy development for underperforming regions.

Analysis Insights:

- Categories such as Art & Architecture show strong quarterly revenue acceleration, peaking in Q4.
- Central and Mid-Atlantic regions exhibit consistent growth, indicating high opportunity for expansion.
- Visual controls help stakeholders easily identify leader products and plan region-specific campaigns.

Management Scorecard

The Management Scorecard dashboard presents a high-level summary of key business metrics by quarter, offering essential data for executives and decision-makers.

Metric Cards Include:

- Revenue: Quarterly revenue trends enable rapid assessment of business momentum.
- Profit: Profit cards track quarterly earnings and spot opportunities for cost optimization.
- Profit Margin: Displays changes in profitability ratio to assess margin improvement.
- Units Sold: Monitors overall sales volume, revealing production and inventory trends.

Analysis Insights:

- Revenue and profit have shown steady improvement each quarter, signifying successful strategic initiatives.
- Profit margin fluctuations flag areas for margin management and guide cost review efforts.
- Units sold trend lines help forecast future demand and highlight operational strengths.
